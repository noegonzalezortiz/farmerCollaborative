Who We Are

Farmigo is a Marketing Enterprise that is key for breaching the gaps between farmers and consumers in the capitol region. Farming in the Capitol Region has seen a massive decline and total cessation in some cases in the production of healthy local grown fresh foods. As a result of, our team has decided to revitalize the industry via significant input of ideas, specifically targeting food insecurity/security. This gave birth to our Marketing Enterprise FARMIGO. As such a mandate was derived to provide market facilitation services to the private sector for the production of organic traditional and non-traditional agricultural produce, facilitate local market development, develop and disseminate post-harvest technology, conduct market research, and provide market intelligence services to farmers was adopted.

Farmigo is cognizant of the importance on healthy fresh organic food consumption and the developing and expanding agriculture business enterprises. Of paramount importance to farmers, agro-processors, and agricultural marketers as a whole is the ability to sustain markets for their produce/products. As such, one critical service that is provided by Farmigo is the collaboration with stakeholders in and around the Capitol Region on the development of Agri-Businesses to realize their full potential.Subsequent to that it is within the interest of Farmigo and the Capitol Region farmers,processors etc. That we provided the following services: Agri- Business Development, Market Extension, Promotion and Marketing, Packaging Facilities and Refrigerated Trucking Services.These services are met via networking with partners with expertise and approval from the FDA.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • To provide market facilitation services to the private sector for the export of non-traditional agricultural produce
  • To provide marketing services for the development of the non-traditional agricultural sector
  • To facilitate local market development
  • To develop and disseminate post-harvest technology.
  • To conduct market research and provide market intelligence services to farmers.
  • To create a greater awareness for Capitol Region products
  • To document farmer’s production data
  • To provide farmers of non-traditional agricultural products with marketing advice, assistance for sourcing supplies, harvesting, cleaning, and packaging.
Our Core values
